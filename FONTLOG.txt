FONTLOG
Doulos SIL Cipher font family
========================


This file provides detailed information on the Doulos SIL Cipher font.
This information should be distributed along with the Doulos SIL Cipher font
and any derivative works.


Basic Font Information
----------------------

Doulos is very similar to Times/Times New Roman, but only has a single 
face - regular. It is intended for use alongside other Times-like fonts where 
a range of styles (italic, bold) are not needed.

The goal for this product was to take a subset of the Doulos SIL font and provide
basic non-complex orthographic support for Indonesia PLUS add support for the
Indonesian Kepatihan and Chinese Jianpu Cipher Music Notation. Primary emphasis
has been on Indonesian Kepatihan although we have attempted to include support
for Chinese Jianpu. 

One font from this typeface family is included in this release:

     * Doulos SIL Cipher Regular

Information for Contributors
----------------------------
We welcome contributions to this font project, such as new glyphs, enhanced 
smart font code, or bug fixes. The best way to begin the process is to file 
an issue in the Github project (https://github.com/silnrsi/font-doulos-sil-cipher) 
or respond to an existing issue and express your interest. Then we can begin 
to correspond with you regarding what all might be required and discuss how 
to best submit your contributions.

To enable us to accept contributions in a way that honors your contribution 
and respects your copyright while preserving long-term flexibility for open 
source licensing, you would also need to agree to the SIL Global Contributor 
License Agreement for Font Software (v1.0) prior to sending us your 
contribution. To read more about this requirement and find out how to 
submit the required form, please visit the CLA information page
(https://software.sil.org/fontcla).
